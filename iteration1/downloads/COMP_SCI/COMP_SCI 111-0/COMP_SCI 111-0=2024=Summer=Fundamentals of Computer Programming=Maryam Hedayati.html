ESSAY QUESTIONS

Back | Top of Page

Please summarize your reaction to this course focusing on the aspects that were most important to you.

Table for Please summarize your reaction to this course focusing on the aspects that were most important to you..
Comments
I was surprised at the variety of content covered in this course, from simple programming concepts, such as what makes a language Turing complete, to more complex data structures, such as binary trees. The course was also accessible to all students, regardless of prior programming knowledge, which made for a smooth learning experience for everyone within my section.
Maybe this course would be more effective as a beginner course, but seeing as most students have prior exposure to other, more established, coding languages, the class feels a bit useless. There are no other uses for Racket, and though it might teach someone, who has never coded before, the needed tools to code, for someone who has already learned python or java, it just feels overly complicated and redundant. That being said, this doesn't feel like it should be a required class.
I think this was overall a pretty easy course. There wasn't anything too challenging. However, I thought it was a good place to start a CS journey.
Coming from other programming languages, DrRacket seemed strange and unintuitive, but the beauty of functional programming really becomes clear if you open yourself up to it! Maryam was great at gently guiding us through that transition and she articulated the concepts very well. If you've never programmed before, this course is excellent at teaching you the basic logic and workflow, which equips you with the knowledge to pick up more robust tools quite quickly.
The teaching was very student focused. The classroom environment was very open and inclusive.
