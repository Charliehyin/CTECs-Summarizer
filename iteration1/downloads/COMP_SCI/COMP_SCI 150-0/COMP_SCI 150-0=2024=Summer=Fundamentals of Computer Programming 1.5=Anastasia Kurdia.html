Please summarize your reaction to this course focusing on the aspects that were most important to you.

Table for Please summarize your reaction to this course focusing on the aspects that were most important to you..
Comments
The course moves incredibly fast. It is a 2 week course, with 3 hours of class every single day. There’s a lab due every night at 9 PM, so you have roughly 9 hours to complete an assignment the normal classes would have a week for. It’s a lot of work, but I think it’s worth it if you can handle it.
I highly recommend taking this class with Professor Kurdia because even though it's only two–weeks long, she does a great job of providing lots of support and organizing the class in a way for you to learn and succeed.
I enjoyed it, but only because I prefer working really hard for a short period of time and then resting. It was basically most of the normal quarter packed into 2 weeks, we had 3 quizzes that weren't bad in terms of difficulty. The labs took up most of my time – they would be due after class at 9pm and I usually spent roughly 4–5 hours doing it after class. If you work hard, it isn't difficult to learn a lot and do well in this class, but you have to be prepared to dedicate a lot of time to this class within the two weeks.
This course was fantastic I learned a ton and it was only over in 2 short weeks
Professor Kurdia is an amazing one. If you're looking forward to pursuing a computer science degree, take CS 150 with Kurdia. I took this course for two weeks, and the work was so intensive, but Kurdia knows how to make everything go smoothly and well. Labs( assignments) were kind of challenging, but she is very helpful in providing feedback for any questions you may have about assignments. We had 3 quizzes as well, and they were not hard if you review slides and do practice quizzes posted on canvas.
